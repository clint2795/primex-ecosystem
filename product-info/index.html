Product and fulfilment information.

Clear details on vial format, support materials, availability, payment, and research-use boundaries before a request is completed.

Product format

Compounds are supplied as lyophilised powder vials unless clearly stated otherwise on the planner, catalogue, request summary, or confirmation message.

Support materials

Bacteriostatic water and other support materials may be included, added separately, or confirmed before payment depending on the request and fulfilment route.

Pricing and payment

Prices shown in the Planner, Extended catalogue, or Request Hub are planning or reference prices. Final payment details are confirmed before completion.

Availability and fulfilment

Some items may be held in limited runs or require restock or sourcing time. Any expected delay is confirmed before payment.

Research use only

Products are supplied for research use only. They are not for human or veterinary use, and PrimeX BioLabs does not provide medical advice.

No usage instructions

PrimeX BioLabs does not provide dosing, administration, reconstitution, preparation, or usage instructions.

The Planner and Request Hub are not checkout pages. No payment is taken through the Planner or Request Hub, and fulfilment, availability, support materials, postage, and payment details are confirmed separately before completion.
